# Business Cases

This folder contains a selection of business case projects and analyses, covering areas such as predictive modeling, data analysis, trends, and performance evaluation.

| No. | Project Name                         | Description                                                                 |
|-----|-------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1   | [churn_analysis](./churn_analysis)                       | Study of customer churn patterns and the factors influencing retention.     |
| 2   | [claims_trends](./claims_trends)                        | Examination of claims trends over time to identify patterns and anomalies. |
| 3   | [errors_in_data](./errors_in_data)                      | Identification and investigation of errors and inconsistencies in trading datasets. |
| 4   | [power_bi_dashboard](./power_bi_dashboard)              | Development of a Power BI dashboard to visualize key business metrics.      |
| 5   | [predictive_modelling_evaluation](./predictive_modelling_evaluation) | Evaluation of predictive models, including A/B test analysis. |
| 6   | [ride_tech_research](./ride_tech_research)              | Research and analysis of trends and innovations in bike-share companies.    |
| 7   | [step_tracking_impact](./step_tracking_impact)          | Assessment of a step-tracking app and its impact on health and behavior.    |
| 8   | [time_series_analysis](./time_series_analysis)          | Analysis of time series data to identify trends, seasonality, and forecasting patterns. |
| 9   | [video_service_ltv](./video_service_cohort_analysis)                | Analysis of customer lifetime value (LTV) for a video service platform.     |
| 10   | [ab_test_desing_analysis](./ab_test_desing_analysis)                | Design and implementation of controlled A/B tests to measure feature impact and user behavior.     |

Each project folder may contain scripts, notebooks, datasets, and documentation relevant to its analysis or modeling tasks.
